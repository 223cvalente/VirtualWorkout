Main Exercises you will participate in


Lifting Weights

For 30 minutes the online class will be lifting weights with a professional instructor. You will get many helpful tips and step by step guide on how to properly do this exercise.

Running on Tredmill

For 30 minutes the online class will be running on a tredmill with a professional instructor. If you do not have a tredmill you can follow the other instructor who will do other exercies for your legs. You will get many helpful tips and step by step guide on how to properly do this exercise.

Stationary Bike

For the final 30 minutes the online class will be using a stationary bike with a professional instructor. If you do not have a stationary bike you can either bike outside or choose another activity. You will get many helpful tips and step by step guide on how to properly do this exercise.
